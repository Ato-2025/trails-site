Why map trust?

Trust in an AI system isn't a single score. It's built through the parts that relate back to it: the team that builds it, the infrastructure it runs on, the public it affects, and others. TRAILS maps each part on its own, so instead of asking is this system trustworthy, you can point to exactly which part is thin, and fix that specific gap.
